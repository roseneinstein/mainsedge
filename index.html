The Scoring Philosophy

Brutal by design. Helpful by intent.

UPSC evaluators are not generous. Neither is MainsEdge. Here's exactly how your score is calculated.

2.0 – 3.0
Generic answer. No facts, no data, no structure, no current affairs. Pure vague statements.
3.0 – 4.5
Some relevant content, minimal data, poor multi-dimensional analysis.
4.5 – 6.0
Decent structure, several facts, schemes cited, average analysis. Misses recent current affairs.
6.0 – 7.5
Strong answer. Specific data, recent events with dates, constitutional provisions, multi-dimensional, well-structured. Maximum possible: 7.5.
